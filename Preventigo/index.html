ABOUT US

We Provide You the best services and make you confident about your product that your product is original and you will not be cheated by anyone. Its our resposbility that we can assure you about the quality of your product and its originality. Our research team has lot of experienced members who nare working on the same day and night just to ensure that your product is original.

Every person in the world should be able to know if the products they use are genuine or not. Our founders have spent their careers finding ways to protect products and have invented a truly secure mobile product authentication solution, so brand owners can fight counterfeiting and restore trust

SECURE MOBILE AUTHENTICATION


With the world’s first and only copy proof QR code in the market, Preventigo protects physical goods from counterfeiting. Preventigo Secure Codes contain a unique fingerprint, which even the best counterfeiters are not able to reproduce. Authentication is made with a mobile phone without the need for a special optic.

EASY & SCALABLE INTEGRATION


Preventigo Secure QR codes are directly printed on your existing packaging, labels, or documents enabling a scalable cost structure. No special equipment, ink, paper required. Preventigo just works.

SUPPLY CHAIN ANALYTICS


Preventigo’s cloud-based solution gives instant supply-chain visibility and allows full control of the supply and distribution chain. Identify counterfeit hotspots, prevent grey market goods, monitor distributors and gain insights on end-buyer behavior and demographic data.

O2O BASED ON TRUST


Start a dialogue with your customer based on trust. Preventigo’s platform acts as a powerful trigger for customers who find your brand relevant, allowing you to understand the full picture and gain actionable insights. Keep your shoppers engaged at point of sale across channels, devices and platforms.

OUR SOLUTION

Implement

The Preventigo printer portal allows packaging and label partners to receive orders from brand owners and integrate secure codes into existing automated product packaging and labeling methods.

Optimize

Scan-data is visualized on Preventigo’s online portal. Access real-time business intelligence to recognize patterns and maximize supply chain activity insights. Pro-actively secure your supply chain from the bottom up.

Protect

Preventigo Secure QR Codes can be easily verified with smartphones through our mobile consumer and B2B apps. Each scan generates immediate data visualized on ScanTrust’s online portal. Users and the brand owner learn instantly when a counterfeit is detected.

Engage

Enhance customer engagement with Preventigo’s mobile platform and scan analytics. Incentivize your customers to scan products and develop a relationship with your brand. Leverage your data to increase purchase intent at point-of-sale.
